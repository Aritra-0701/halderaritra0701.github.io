Marbella Wedding Venues

Our speciality is weddings in private and exclusive venues such as villas, Fincas and cortijos. There is a huge variety of incredible private Marbella wedding venues, and it is the perfect way to create a laid-back, more informal and cost-effective wedding. We have an amazing portfolio of venues which we offer in our free Wedding Brochure. We offer a free venue-finding service for any couples visiting the area. We meet at our wedding showroom Fiestasol HQ and take couples out on a day of site visits to make sure you find your dream Marbella wedding venue.

Marbella wedding villas

Holding your wedding in a private villa is a very popular and more economical option. See our Marbella wedding villa page for more information. Whether you are looking for a modern luxury private villa or a traditional Spanish Finca, there is a huge selection of venues to suit all styles and budgets. Some venues are available for exclusive one-day hire and some include accommodation and are available for 3 days or more. We have a great selection of hotels, villas and apartments close to all our main wedding venues that we can offer for you, your wedding party and your guests.
